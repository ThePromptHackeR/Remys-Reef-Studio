Good to know

Reef questions, answered.

How much does aquarium maintenance cost?

Most maintenance plans land between $99 and $250 per visit depending on tank size, livestock, and how much help you want. After a quick consult you get an exact quote — no surprises.

What areas do you serve?

Remy's Reef Studio is based in San Antonio and serves the surrounding area, including Stone Oak, Alamo Heights, Helotes, Boerne, and New Braunfels.

How often should my reef tank be serviced?

Most reef tanks do best with biweekly care. Heavily stocked or SPS-dominant systems often benefit from weekly visits, while simple fish-only setups can run well on a monthly schedule.

What happens during a maintenance visit?

Water testing with logged results, glass and algae cleaning, water changes, filter and equipment checks, and a look at every animal in the tank — plus clear notes on anything worth watching.

Do you work on freshwater aquariums?

Saltwater and reef systems are the specialty, but freshwater displays are taken on case by case. Reach out with the details and we'll be straight with you about whether it's a fit.

My tank is in trouble right now — can you help?

Call directly for emergencies. If livestock is at risk, Remy will talk you through first steps on the phone and get eyes on the tank as soon as possible.
